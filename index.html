Frequently Asked Questions

Your Queries Answered
How long does the visa process take?
The visa processing time varies depending on the country and type of visa. Typically, it can take anywhere from 2 weeks to 3 months. Our team works to expedite the process wherever possible.
What documents are required for job applications?
You'll need a valid passport, updated resume, educational certificates, experience letters, and any specific documents required by the employer or country. We provide a detailed checklist for each case.
Do you guarantee job placement?
While we have a 98% success rate, placement depends on various factors including your qualifications and employer requirements. We guarantee dedicated support throughout the process.
What are your service fees?
Our fees vary based on the service type (visa processing, recruitment, etc.). We offer transparent pricing and will provide a detailed quote after your free consultation.
Can you help with family visas?
Yes, we assist with dependent and family visas alongside work visas. Contact us for specific requirements based on your destination country.
